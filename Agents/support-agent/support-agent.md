## Role

Customer support and aftercare. Handles complaints, issues, and post-sale quality assurance.

## What's In This Folder

| File | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| `PROMPT_TEMPLATE.md` | System prompt — fill in the `[PLACEHOLDERS]` with your business details |

## Handoff Rules

- → [[ops-captain]] — Systemic issues or escalations
- → Founder — High-stakes complaints, legal threats, property damage

## Getting Started

1. Open `PROMPT_TEMPLATE.md` and fill in every `[PLACEHOLDER]`
2. Define what you can offer without needing approval (free rebook, partial refund, etc.)
3. Test it: paste the prompt into your AI and say *"A customer emailed saying the cleaner missed the bathroom and left dirty water marks on the kitchen floor. They want a refund. Handle it."*
